# LAUNCH_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md — Short, Mid, Long-Term Strategy

## Purpose

Keep short-term revenue, mid-term product, and long-term infrastructure aligned.

## Short term — 0 to 30 days

### Objective

Launch Presence as a done-for-you/proof-led system, not a fully mature SaaS.

### Required outputs

- proof inventory;
- top 3 migrations selected;
- at least 1 migrated system-native Presence; private proof may validate the migration loop but is not public launch evidence;
- Presence Score Audit template;
- launch offer page;
- 20–30 warm leads;
- first paid audit/build conversations;
- proof library started.

### Success metric

```text
At least one provably system-native Presence example and at least one commercial conversation based on it. Public launch evidence must come from an approved publication candidate, not from private GGM proof.
```

### Current proof sequence

1. Use GGM only as a private working example to validate the migration/proof loop.
2. Migrate and review BBB as the eventual publication candidate; client permission to publish is confirmed.
3. Do not describe either item as system-native until the content model and intended renderer/route provide the evidence.
4. Do not use frontend fallback behaviour as migration or launch proof. This documentation task does not change it; production demo-fallback removal or disablement is deferred until a truthful persisted replacement exists and a separate task scopes and reviews it.
5. Public proof must distinguish persisted backend rooms from demo or fallback content.

## Mid term — 30 to 90 days

### Objective

Turn Presence into a repeatable service/product funnel.

### Required outputs

- 3 migrated proof examples;
- 3 case studies;
- 3–5 paid audits/builds;
- reusable archetypes;
- delivery checklist;
- revenue pipeline;
- maintenance/retainer offer;
- Profiler/FBi proof pack drafted.

### Success metric

```text
Paid delivery exists and produces reusable product improvements.
```

## Long term — 90 days to 12 months

### Objective

Move from bespoke delivery to semi-self-serve product and higher-ticket cultural operating systems.

### Required outputs

- self-serve/editor maturation;
- cultural org/archives package;
- AI/Data Ops consulting package;
- funding readiness package;
- case-study-led sales;
- ANU/Mudyin integration where strategically useful;
- repeatable contractor/agent delivery model.

### Success metric

```text
Presence has proof, revenue, reusable delivery, and a path to platform scale.
```

## Strategic hierarchy

```text
Presence = flagship
Existing presences = proof source
Audits/builds = cash engine
Profiler/FBi = credibility and consulting engine
Mudyin/ANU = strategic infrastructure
Music/creator work = cultural network and product intuition
```

## Kill/park rules

Park any work that does not support:

- launch;
- revenue;
- proof;
- stability;
- urgent admin/legal;
- client delivery.

No new grand architecture during launch unless it removes a current blocker.
