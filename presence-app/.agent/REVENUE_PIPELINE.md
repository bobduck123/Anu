## Outreach rules

Agents may draft. Humans send.

Do not:
- spam;
- overclaim;
- use fake scarcity;
- mention private/sensitive client work;
- pitch full platform if only done-for-you builds are ready.

## Outreach message skeleton

```text
Hey <name> — I’m opening a small number of done-for-you Presence builds before the wider launch.

The idea is a stronger digital home than a link-in-bio or generic site: something that makes the project feel real, memorable, and easy to understand.

I’m also doing quick Presence Score audits where I review what’s currently online and give a practical rebuild plan.

Thought you/this project could be a strong fit because <specific reason>.

Would you want me to take a look?
```

- invoice/payment tracking;
