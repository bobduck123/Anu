# Hosted Smoke Steps

Run after deployment against a prepared owner room. Use reversible text and image selections.

1. In a fresh private browser session, sign in once as the room owner.
2. Open `/studio/{roomId}/editor` and confirm Build is default, with Draft/Live status visible.
3. Confirm Advanced is demoted and no technical field language appears in the normal shell.
4. Edit a visible title to `EDITOR UX V15 SMOKE [timestamp]` and save.
5. Open Look and change one reversible font or mood choice; save.
6. Open Images and confirm the Media drawer, image roles, and honest Upload coming soon panel.
7. Choose an existing safe room image and edit alt text; save.
8. Open the anonymous public room and confirm the marker and draft image change are absent.
9. Open private preview and confirm the marker, chosen image, and style change are present.
10. Return to Studio, click Open room to visitors, confirm the dialog copy, and publish deliberately.
11. Open the public room anonymously and confirm the published changes.
12. Open RoomKey and confirm it reflects only the published state.
13. Restore the original text, image, alt text, font/mood and publish that cleanup.
14. Confirm the public room and RoomKey show the restored state.

Record screenshots for each lifecycle boundary and confirm no owner/private metadata appears in public HTML.
