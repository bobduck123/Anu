# Presence Ecosystem Tiers and Pricing

Pricing is a product reference, not billing logic in alpha.

## 0. Showcase / Starter Profile

- Beautiful profile, links, simple contact, QR, vCard, SEO.
- Demo, pilot, community access, or discounted wedge.

## 1. Presence Opportunity Kit

- Boutique profile, professional bio, service/offer section, enquiry flow, QR/vCard/share, SEO/social preview, basic analytics, admin-editable content, readiness flags, one opportunity module.
- Reference: setup plus recurring support/hosting.

## 2. Professional Contract Presence

- Capability statement, offers, proof ledger, procurement profile, advanced deal intake, PDF/private deal room extension points, analytics, directory/referral readiness.
- Target: consultants, fractional executives, advisors, senior specialists.

## 3. Artist / Practitioner Presence

- Gallery or practitioner microsite, optional landing portal, practice/curatorial statements, works, collections, services, booking/intake readiness, press/EPK readiness.

## 4. Tradie / Field Service Presence

- Public trade profile, licences/certifications, service area, quote request, before/after gallery, quote/variation/invoice/handover foundations, NFC source tracking, relationship ledger, referral/review loop extension point.

## 5. Organisation / Venue Presence

- Organisation or venue profile, team/practitioner node relationships readiness, programs/services, gallery/media, enquiry routing, sponsorship/funder/grant/impact readiness, directory/map/archive readiness.

## 6. White-label Network

- Multi-node branded ecosystem, tenant-ready services, organisation dashboard, public directory/network analytics foundations.
